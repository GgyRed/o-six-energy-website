About O-Six Energy Limited

O-Six Energy Limited is a high-rising engineering servicing firm focusing on the civil construction of durable mini-grid and off-grid solar renewable energy infrastructures for global renewable energy companies.

Operating across Nigeria and Sub-Saharan Africa, O-Six Energy ensures every project is built on solid foundations — from PV bases to equipment sheds, perimeter fencing, and all civil works that power renewable installations.

Our team has a combined experience of over 25 years in the energy and construction industries, enabling us to deliver quality that sustains the renewable future.

Our Mission

To provide world-class civil engineering support for renewable energy infrastructure projects, ensuring durability, safety, and environmental harmony across Africa’s growing clean energy landscape.

Our Vision

To be the most trusted engineering partner for renewable energy companies in Africa — known for precision, reliability, and excellence in sustainable infrastructure delivery.

Core Values

  • Integrity: We operate with transparency and responsibility.
  • Quality: Our work stands the test of time.
  • Innovation: We adapt modern engineering to local realities.
  • Collaboration: Partnering with global firms for shared success.
  • Sustainability: Every project contributes to a greener planet.
