Our Services

Civil Construction for Renewable Mini-Grids

We provide specialized civil construction services that form the foundation of renewable mini-grid projects. Our work ensures that energy infrastructure is built on safe, stable, and durable foundations suitable for long-term operation.

  • Site clearing and preparation
  • Earthworks and excavation
  • Structural foundations for energy assets
  • Access roads, fencing, and compound works

Transmission & Distribution Infrastructure

O-Six Energy delivers transmission and distribution services that enable the safe and efficient delivery of electricity across communities, facilities, and businesses.

  • Pole erection and mounting
  • Conductor stringing
  • Low-tension (LT) distribution networks
  • High-tension (HT) line installations

Solar Solutions for Homes & Businesses

We design and install solar energy systems that provide reliable power for residential, commercial, and institutional clients seeking clean and cost-effective energy solutions.

  • Energy needs assessment
  • System design and sizing
  • Solar installation and commissioning
  • Battery storage integration
