Mini-Grid Construction Project

Project Overview

This project showcases the construction of a durable mini-grid base in a rural Sub-Saharan community. Our team provided full civil works — including foundation setting, PV structure installation, and equipment housing. The project stands as a testament to O-Six Energy Limited’s engineering excellence and commitment to building the foundation for renewable energy across Africa.
